Our missions

Safe Community

We keep our users safer because only students from KMITL can access this app.

Save Cost

This app allows you to share the cost of gas and parking, cutting your expenses by nearly 50% or more; the more occupants in your carpool the more you save.

Save Earth

Having fewer cars on the road every day, which means fewer carbons being released into the air. A reduced amount of carbon emissions will help reduce air pollution as well.
